Our Story

At Merriment, we believe that celebration is a holy act. Founded with the mission to bring “true merriment” to gatherings, we help families and parishes create events that are both beautiful and spirit-filled.

From the solemn beauty of a Nuptial Mass to the joyful chaos of a parish picnic, we handle the logistics so you can focus on the grace of the moment.

Why “Merriment”?

“Merriment” generally suggests light-hearted gaiety and fun. But for us, it roots in the deep, abiding joy that comes from community and sacrament. As G.K. Chesterton might say, “Joy, which was the small publicity of the pagan, is the gigantic secret of the Christian.”
